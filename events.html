Why Attend?

What Is a Trunk Show?

A trunk show is a special event where a designer sends their complete collection to our boutique for a limited time. This means you get access to gowns that are not normally available in-store, giving you far more options to find your perfect dress.

During trunk shows, designers often offer exclusive pricing and incentives that are only available during the event. It is the best time to order your dream dress at a special price.

Whether you are just beginning your dress search or have been looking for that one special gown, a trunk show gives you the widest selection and the best value. Appointments fill up quickly, so we recommend booking early.

Why Brides Love Trunk Shows

  • ✿ Access to the full designer collection
  • ✿ Exclusive event-only pricing
  • ✿ Try on styles not normally in-store
  • ✿ Expert guidance from our stylists
  • ✿ First look at newest designs
  • ✿ A fun and memorable experience
