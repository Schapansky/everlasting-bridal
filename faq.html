Common Questions

Yes, bridal appointments are required to ensure you receive our full, undivided attention. We dedicate each appointment exclusively to one bride at a time, so you can explore our collection in a private, relaxed setting. You can book your appointment online through our Appointments page, by calling us at (678) 635-8937, or by emailing shopeverlastingbridal@gmail.com.
We recommend bringing up to 4 guests to your bridal appointment. Our intimate boutique setting is designed to give you and your closest loved ones a comfortable, personal experience. Keeping your group small helps ensure that the focus stays on you and that everyone can enjoy the moment together without feeling crowded.
We carry sample gowns in sizes 2 through 28, and our designers offer made-to-order gowns in a wide range of sizes to ensure a perfect fit for every bride. During your appointment, our stylists will help you find styles that flatter your figure and can discuss custom sizing options available through our designer partners.
Most wedding gowns take approximately 4 to 6 months from the time of order to arrive. We recommend shopping for your dress at least 8 to 10 months before your wedding date to allow time for ordering, shipping, and any alterations. If your wedding is sooner, ask us about rush ordering options or our in-stock gowns that are available for immediate purchase.
While we do not perform alterations in-house, we have trusted alterations partners that we highly recommend. These skilled seamstresses specialize in bridal alterations and work closely with our team to ensure your gown fits perfectly. We are happy to connect you with our recommended partners when you purchase your dress.
All sales are final on ordered wedding gowns, as each dress is custom ordered specifically for you. We understand this is a significant purchase, which is why our stylists work with you to make sure you are completely in love with your gown before placing the order. Exchanges are available on accessories within 14 days of purchase, provided they are in original condition with tags attached.
Yes, we offer tuxedo and suit rentals with convenient in-store fittings. Our collection includes a range of classic and modern styles perfect for grooms, groomsmen, fathers of the bride, and any formal occasion. We handle measurements, ordering, and coordination so the entire wedding party looks sharp on the big day.
We offer a professional gown preservation service to keep your wedding dress beautiful for years to come. After your wedding, bring your gown to us and our preservation experts will carefully clean, treat, and package it in an acid-free preservation box. This process protects against yellowing, fabric deterioration, and damage, keeping your dress as stunning as the day you wore it. Contact us for pricing and details.
