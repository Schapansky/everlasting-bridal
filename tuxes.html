Our Tuxedo Services

Rentals, Purchases & Group Fittings

Whether you are looking to rent a tuxedo for the evening or invest in a suit you will wear for years, our expert fitters will ensure a flawless look. We carry premium designer brands and offer a seamless fitting experience.

Rental Packages — Choose from our curated collection of designer tuxedos, available for rental with all the essentials: jacket, pants, shirt, vest or cummerbund, tie, and cufflinks.

Purchase Options — Looking for something to keep? We offer a selection of suits and tuxedos available for purchase, tailored to your exact measurements.

Groomsmen Group Fittings — Bring the entire wedding party in for a group fitting session. We coordinate styles, sizes, and accessories to ensure a cohesive, polished look for the entire group.

Designer Brands — We partner with top formalwear designers to bring you the latest styles, premium fabrics, and impeccable tailoring at every price point.
