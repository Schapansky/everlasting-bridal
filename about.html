About Everlasting Bridal

Where Every Love Story Begins

Everlasting Bridal opened its doors in June 2021, nestled inside the beautifully restored historic Monroe Cotton Mill. From the very beginning, we were founded on a simple but powerful belief: every bride deserves personalized, one-on-one attention during one of the most important shopping experiences of her life.

Located at 600 S. Broad St. Ste. A200, Monroe, GA 30655, our boutique offers an intimate setting that feels worlds away from the overwhelming chaos of larger bridal stores. When you step through our doors, you are our only priority. There are no distractions, no crowds — just you, your closest loved ones, and a dedicated stylist focused entirely on helping you find the dress of your dreams.

We believe that finding the perfect wedding dress should be a joyful, memorable experience. Our expert stylists take the time to listen to your vision, understand your style, and guide you through our carefully curated collection of designer gowns. Every detail matters to us, because every bride who walks through our doors deserves to feel absolutely special.

Our commitment goes beyond the dress. From your first appointment to your wedding day and beyond — with gown preservation services to protect your memories — Everlasting Bridal is here to make your bridal journey as beautiful as the love story you are celebrating.

Personalized Experience

Your appointment is exclusively yours. We dedicate our full attention to you and your guests, creating a private and relaxed environment where you can explore styles, try on gowns, and truly enjoy the moment without feeling rushed or overlooked.

Expert Stylists

Our knowledgeable stylists bring years of bridal expertise to every appointment. They understand fabrics, silhouettes, and design details, and they use that knowledge to help you discover gowns you might never have considered — often finding "the one" in the most unexpected places.

Designer Collections

We carry a hand-selected collection from world-renowned designers including Maggie Sottero, Allure Bridals, Allure Couture, and Sottero & Midgley. Each gown in our boutique has been chosen for its exceptional craftsmanship, luxurious fabrics, and timeless beauty.
