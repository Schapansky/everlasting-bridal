Your Appointment Experience

Your bridal appointment at Everlasting Bridal is a private, one-on-one experience lasting 60 to 90 minutes. From the moment you arrive, you will be paired with a dedicated stylist who will guide you through every step.

  • ✿ Personal stylist assigned to you
  • ✿ 60 – 90 minute private session
  • ✿ Bring your inspiration photos
  • ✿ Up to 4 guests welcome
  • ✿ Complimentary refreshments
  • ✿ No pressure, no rush
